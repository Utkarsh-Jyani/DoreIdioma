Why we created this website ?

  • We are aware communication plays a vital role in every individual’s life, some studies have even shown 85% of success is attributed to communication skills and 15% to mastery of work skills.
  • Unfortunately, many of us don’t have the ability to do so. Deaf-Mute or Deaf-dumb people find it pretty challenging to communicate with fellow humans on a day-to-day basis.
  • Staying alongside every time to help them is the second-best way but the first is to let them be independent with a little less aid.
  • The most suitable approach that we came up with is to provide them a website comprising of AI fielded features, involving basic functioning to complex tasks.
